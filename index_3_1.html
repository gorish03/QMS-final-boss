Who We Are

India's Trusted Quality Management Partner

QMS India Limited has grown from a specialized management consultancy firm into a leading organization in the fields of sustainability, safety, environment, quality, and software solutions. We have expanded our presence across India, serving industries ranging from automotive and mining to aviation and government infrastructure.

With a team of skilled engineers and certified experts, we excel as service providers in management standards, safety inspection, training, testing, auditing, and software solutions. Our practical and innovative approaches are meticulously tailored to achieve measurable goals, giving our clients a competitive edge.

Our extensive presence spans across India. We specialize in technology-driven solutions that fuel transformation and enhance productivity.
